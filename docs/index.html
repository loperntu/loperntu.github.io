謝舒凱 Shu-Kai Hsieh (IPA: /ɕi̯ɛ⁵¹/)

Professor  ·  國立台灣大學 語言學研究所 / 腦與心智科學研究所

I am a joint-appointed professor at the Graduate Institute of Linguistics and the Institute of Brain and Mind Sciences at National Taiwan University, Taiwan.

My work sits at the intersection of computational linguistics, language resources, and the quest to understand how language shapes — and is shaped by — cognition, culture, and consciousness. I lead the LOPE Laboratory.

At my spare time I enjoy reading Buddhist scriptures, play instruments, and practice Chinese calligraphy.
